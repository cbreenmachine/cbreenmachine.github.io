Statistical Consulting

My favorite part of data science is doing full-stack statistical analysis: working with a client in identifying/refining hypotheses, fitting/evaluating models, and building a narrative to tie it all together. A couple of representative reports are found below.
  • Quantifying the cold hardiness of trees in the American Northeast.
    • 30,000 ft view plots made the problem much easier to understand
    • Data quality control was critical (small labeling errors in the raw data would have caused problems)
    • Mixed-effects models are great in this context
  • Assessing evidence of an exoplanet transit
    • Clean visualizations made the problem setup pop
    • Simple (Gaussian mixture) models provided the perfect amount of interpretability
    • Interpretation was critical!
  • Comparing two modeling paradigms to map hyperspectral imaging data to foliar traits
    • Comparing Partial Least Squares and Bayesian Spike-and-slab regression
    • Using hyperspectral imaging data as input and foliar traits (e.g. chemical composition) as output
    • Feature selection facilitates interpretability
